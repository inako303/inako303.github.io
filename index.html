植物伴侣

Plant Mate

该项目探索了一个生物未来的讨论空间，质询当下科学技术的发展在未来会呈现怎样的影响，并重新审视人类与其他物种之间的关系。

The project explores a space for discussion of the biological future, questioning how current developments in science and technology will present implications in the future and re-examining the relationship between humans and other species.


在基因编辑已经成熟的未来语境下，一种新型服务行业应运而生——基于沉默突变原理，科研人员可以在不影响植物基因的情况下存储信息，将人类的DNA载入至植物体内——植物可以承载你已故爱人/亲人/朋友的DNA，他们将以另一种生命形式继续存在。

In a future context where gene editing is ripe for the picking, a new service industry has emerged - based on the principle of silencing mutations, researchers can store information without affecting plant genes and load human DNA into plants - plants can carry the DNA of your deceased loved one/relative/friend and they will continue to exist in another life form.


从神经生物学角度看，植物和人很相像，尽管植物并没有真正的神经和大脑，但它们可以通过生物电信号的变化来感知和传递信息，甚至有自己的偏好和记忆。植物反应迟缓、无法移动，无法与人进行有效互动和反馈。于是我们探讨是否存在一种方式，可以将人类行为与植物在它们世界中活动的方式等同起来。我们找寻了动植物之间的共同需求，从感受植物的生命体征、感受植物的感受、转译对话、感官转换四个维度，重新建立情境意识，为其注入多样性、亲密感和包容性的空间，寻求更隐秘的情感认同。

From a neurobiological point of view, plants are very similar to humans. Although plants do not have real nerves or brains, they can sense and transmit information through changes in bioelectrical signals and even have their own preferences and memories. Plants are slow to respond, unable to move, and unable to interact and give feedback effectively with people. So we explored whether there was a way to equate human behaviour with the way plants move through their world. We searched for common needs between plants and animals, in four dimensions: feeling the plant's vital signs, feeling the plant's feelings, translating dialogue, and sensory transformation, to re-establish situational awareness and to infuse space for diversity, intimacy and inclusivity, seeking a more implicit emotional identity.


该作品提出了一系列人类和植物交互转译的设计理念。基于植物通过生物电信号的变化来感知和传递信息的特性，该装置将生物电信号转化为可感知的媒介：气、雾、光、气泡、波纹、声音等，放大微观世界中的植物行为。其功能就像小道具一样帮助人们展开想象，建立物种间的感性对话。我们看到了人类演化的另一种可能结局，一种生物平行关系下的物种间的新型关系。
The work proposes a series of design concepts for human-plant interaction and translation. Based on the nature of plants to perceive and transmit information through changes in bioelectrical signals, the installation translates bioelectrical signals into perceptible media: air, mist, light, bubbles, ripples, sound, etc., amplifying plant behaviour in the microcosm. It functions like a small prop to help people develop their imagination and establish a perceptual dialogue between species. We see another possible end to human evolution, a new type of relationship between species in a biologically parallel relationship.

设计虚构
Design Fiction

影片讲述了一个男人因无法承受突然失去妻子的痛苦，向 "植物伴侣中心 "发出了植物请求。结果，他收到了一株携带他亡妻DNA的植物，而他的妻子则以另一种生命形式继续存在。该男子在轻松愉快的日常中与植物互动，感受植物的呼吸和生命体征，通过七个小道具与它对话......仿佛他的妻子还在他身边......

The film tells the story of a man who, unable to bear the sudden loss of his wife, sends a plant request to the "Plant Companion Centre". As a result, he receives a plant carrying his late wife's DNA, and his wife continues to exist in another form of life. The man interacts with the plant in a relaxed and enjoyable daily routine, feeling the plant's breathing and vital signs, talking to it through seven small props ...... It is as if his wife is still with him ......

作品介绍
Works

The logic corresponding to each product
戳戳

戳戳 - 植物内部的各种信号在传达它的健康状态，该装置提取有关植物生长状态的信息，并将其转化为气流来控制小球的高度。人们可以与植物进行双向互动，戳戳小球，根据弹跳的力度了解植物的健康状况。

Poking 

Poking - Various signals inside the plant are communicating its health status and the device extracts information about the plant's growth status and translates it into airflow to control the height of the small ball. People can interact with the plant in both directions, poking the ball and learning the health of the plant based on the strength of the bounce.

陀螺

陀螺--人类和植物都有控制生物钟的隐花色素的蓝光受体。当色素受体收到光信号时，陀螺仪会随着植物的昼夜节律缓慢旋转，并在空间中折射光线。希望光斑的变化和小球的运动轨迹能够告诉人们，植物何时在早晨接受第一道红光，在晚上接受第一道远红光。

Gyroscope 

Gyroscope - Both humans and plants have blue light receptors for cryptoglobin, which control the biological clock. When the pigment receptors receive light signals, the gyroscope slowly rotates with the plant's circadian rhythm and refracts the light in space. It is hoped that the change in the light spot and the position of the gyroscope's movement will tell people when the plant receives the first red light in the morning versus the first far-red light at night. 

转转

转转--呼吸是人与植物的共同需求，人类和植物正在进行着氧气与二氧化碳的相互转换。转动速率依据植物的呼吸速率，感受植物的生命体征。调节身体动作与它们同步，进行冥想放松，以达到一种呼吸同频。

Turning  

Turning -Breathing is a common need for both humans and plants, and humans and plants are undergoing an interchange of oxygen and carbon dioxide. The rate of rotation is based on the breathing rate of the plant, and the vital signs of the plant are felt. Adjust your body movements to synchronise with them and engage in meditation and relaxation to achieve a breathing synchronicity.

波纹

波纹 — 植物在缺水、茎被切断或其它舒适环境下，由于空化的内部过程，植物会发出人耳听不见的超声波。通过音流学将其转化为可视的波纹，它们反映植物受不同胁迫下的状态，随着波纹大小与形状的变化，建立人与植物的“对话”。

Corrugated  

Corrugated - When plants are deprived of water, have their stems cut off, or are in other comfortable environments, they emit ultrasonic waves that are inaudible to the human ear due to the internal process of cavitation. Through phonorheology, these are transformed into visible ripples that reflect the state of the plant under different stresses, creating a "dialogue" between humans and plants as the size and shape of the ripples change.

技术
Technology


利用max程序，生成不同频率hz的超声波，利用音流学原理。将人耳听不见的声音以波纹形式可视化。
Using the max program, ultrasound waves of different frequency hz are generated, using the principle of phonetic flow. Visualisation of sounds that are inaudible to the human ear in the form of ripples.

嘟嘟嘟

嘟嘟嘟 — 植物的根细胞吸收了大量的矿物元素，同时光合作用导致植物内部元素离子的分布不平衡，使植物内部存在运动的电荷。将该生物电转化为舵的上下运动，并带有音乐和律动，就像一个生物电乐器，人们可以感受到植物的生命节奏，并跟随着它摇摆。

Dodo

Dodo — The root cells of the plant absorb a large amount of mineral elements, while photosynthesis leads to an imbalance in the distribution of elemental ions within the plant, giving rise to an electric charge of movement within the plant. Translating this bioelectricity into an up and down movement of the rudder with a musical and rhythmic sound, like a bioelectric instrument, one can feel the rhythm of the plant's life and sway to it.

雾 

雾--植物的根部形成一个复杂的网状结构，探测土地并分析环境。该装置通过雾气反映出植物所感知的空气和土壤的环境湿度。随着雾气的变化，人们可以感受植物的感受。

Fogging 

Fogging-The roots of the plant form a complex mesh that detects the land and analyses the environment. The device reflects the ambient humidity of the air and soil as perceived by the plant through the mist. As the mist changes, one can feel what the plant is feeling.

泡泡

泡泡--植物的茎负责运输水。为了让水在内部流动，木质部中会产生气泡。植物木质部中气泡的产生和消失可以反映植物内部的水分运输情况。气泵模拟植物木质部中气泡的运动，告诉人们它可能需要水。

bubbles

bubbles - The stems of plants are responsible for transporting water. In order to allow water to flow internally, air bubbles are produced in the xylem. The production and disappearance of air bubbles in the xylem of a plant can reflect the water transport within the plant. An air pump simulates the movement of bubbles in the xylem of a plant, telling people that it may need water.
